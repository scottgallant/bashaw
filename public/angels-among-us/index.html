Angels Among Us

A heavenly community celebration & fundraiser.
December 12, 2015
Bashaw United Church

Step 1

Take a picture of any Act of Angels you’ve performed or witnessed.

Step 2

Post it to Facebook or Instagram with the hashtag #BashawAngels. Or, pick up an Angel Card around town and fill it out.

Step 3

Stop at the Bashaw United Church to see the Acts of Angels outdoor exhibit we’ll be creating out of everyone’s Angel Cards!

Angel Fest 2015

8:30 a.m. Check In Angel Fun Run & Walk - Registration is $25 (preschoolers FREE)
10:00 a.m. Start Strap on your wings, shine up your halo and make your way through our community angel route. Prizes for top pledge earners and a souvenir toque for every participant.
10:00 a.m. Silent Angel Auction
Angel themed silent auction items on display for bidding all day.
10:00 a.m. - 3:00 p.m. Angel Photo Booth
Bring your friends and family and pose together in our angel photo booth after finding the perfect angel gear in our costume trunk! Angel Food Bake Sale
Stock up on some heavenly Christmas goodies at our special Angel Food Bake Sale.
4:00 p.m. Angels Assemble!
Take a seat and watch the cutest actors you’ve ever seen take the stage in this angel themed Christmas theatre production.
5:00 p.m. Angel Hair Spaghetti Supper
End the day with one final stroll through the silent auction items at the church and warm up with a delicious noodle and sauce extravaganza!

All funds raised will go directly to the Bashaw United Church Community Impact Fund, ensuring that programs, volunteers and space are provided for the community in areas that encourage compassion, understanding, courage, love, appreciation and teamwork.
