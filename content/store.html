Children are an important part of our congregation!!

On sunday mornings, we gather as the Whole People of God. Our opening prayers, hymns and songs reflect the diversity of our ages and backgrounds - that "we who are many and come from many places" have gathered as a community. There is time for a story or activity that introduces the theme and key scripture passage for the day. We call this time Learning Together because it is not just time for the children, but for the whole congregation to learn and experience God.

After a song or hymn, the children go downstairs or outside. They’ll hear the same scripture story the adults will and engage it in prayer and discussion with a creative activity that connects to the theme of the day. Art, drama, song and play are all part of the morning.

The children also occasionally lead the Sunday morning service for everyone, present an annual Christmas pageant or drama and have after school events, like Messy Church. Our Movie Night series includes a Family Movie each month.

Our week-long summer program for children usually runs in the third week of July each year.

3 NEW RESOURCES FOR CHILDREN’S MINISTRY

Versatile five session programs for children aged 5-12.

Use them for

  • A one week summer or vacation program
  • Church school 
  • A weekly program

GOSPEL ACCORDING TO SEUSS

Get Suess-ed for a week! Six stories by Theodore Geisel are the basis for exploring how Jesus teaches us to live God’s way: Oh, the Thinks you can Think!, Horton hears a Who!, The Sneetches, Green Eggs and Ham, The Lorax and Oh, the Places You’ll Go!

Suggested songs from Voices United, More Voices

and All God’s Children Sing. (33 page PDF)

TRAIL OF ADVENTURE

Five Bible stories re-told with a western flavour highlight this week-long journey about God’s faith in us, our faith in God and our love for one another: Moses and the Big Drive (Exodus 20), Daniel Do-Right in the Bull Corral (Daniel 6:4-27), Good Guy Sam (Luke 10:25-37), The Legend of Pappy’s Gold-en Onions (Luke 12:13-21) and God’s Pony Express (John 13:34-35).

There’s songs with familiar old-time country tunes and some wild west crafts. (51 page PDF)

MAKE ME: A Kid's Week of Creation

The creation story in Genesis is the basis for exploring our relationship with God and the world around us using our own creativity. Includes an original re-telling of the creation, Adam and Eve’s adventures and their departure from the garden. I am the earth, the earth is me and What a wonderful world are key song themes and the crafts are, well, all about being creative. (42 page PDF)
